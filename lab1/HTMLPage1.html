The routine

This is a routine for 5 days in a week.

Day Workout
Monday Push
Tuesday Pull
Wednesday Legs
Thursday Push
Friday Pull

Push=Chest+Triceps+Shoulders

Pull=Back+Biceps

Cable Pushdowns 4x 10-12 Cable Biceps Curls 4x 10 Oblique Extansions 4x 10-12 Dips 5x max Leg Raises 5x 12-15

Compound exercises

In every day we encourage you to focus on a compound exercise.

  • Day1 Bench press
  • Day2 Pull-ups
  • Day3 Squats
  • Day4 Military press
  • Day5 Deadlift

Also is a good practice to have a cardio session every week.

If you don't have motivation to start working out maybe you will find some motivation here.
